About Me

I am proud to say that Practicum changed my life. I went from elementary school teacher to web developer. I started a very successful startup that I was able to sell to a tech giant, and have been relaxing in the Big Easy, freelancing. I aspire to be a software engineer with NASA!

I am an active volunteer with the Google Developer’s Group (GDG) and often organize tech events and meetups for developers of all skill levels. I’ve been mentoring and have taken many students under my wings. Through it all: I keep up with learning new technologies and keeping up to date with the one’s I’ve arleady learned!

When I’m between projects, I’m constantly outside: hiking, camping, skiing. I practice Wing Chun, and play with my chickens and pet ferret.
